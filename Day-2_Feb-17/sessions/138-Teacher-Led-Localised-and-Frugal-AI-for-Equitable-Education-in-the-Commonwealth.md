## üìù Summary

This session presents practical pathways for "Teacher-in-the-Loop" AI and localized, open-source LLM deployments. Commonwealth ministers and leaders will examine policy, capacity, and governance to enable frugal, sovereign, and inclusive AI in education, specifically for lower-resource settings. Key outcomes include identifying pilot collaborations, publishing a sovereign education-AI reference architecture, and establishing monitorable indicators for 12-month follow-up.

## üîë Key Takeaways

1. This session presents practical pathways for "Teacher-in-the-Loop" AI and localized, open-source LLM deployments.
2. Commonwealth ministers and leaders will examine policy, capacity, and governance to enable frugal, sovereign, and inclusive AI in education, specifically for lower-resource settings.
3. Key outcomes include identifying pilot collaborations, publishing a sovereign education-AI reference architecture, and establishing monitorable indicators for 12-month follow-up.
