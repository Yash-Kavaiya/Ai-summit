## üìù Summary

"AI for Disability Inclusion: Global Lessons on Accessibility by Design" examines how artificial intelligence can advance disability inclusion through an accessibility-by-design approach. Convened by The Quantum Hub (TQH), the Royal Society (UK), and the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, Government of India, it brings together leaders from government, industry, and civil society. Drawing on global evidence, the discussion will focus on co-designing AI solutions with persons with disabilities and on policy safeguards aligned with India's social empowerment priorities.
