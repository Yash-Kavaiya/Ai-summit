## üìù Summary

This high-level roundtable convenes parliamentarians, diplomats, multilateral organizations, and technology governance experts to facilitate a strategic dialogue on shaping an inclusive global AI agenda rooted in the developmental priorities of the Global South. Participants will explore frameworks for multilateral cooperation and examine how legislative and regulatory ecosystems can encourage responsible AI innovation in critical domains including healthcare, agriculture, education, and social protection, ensuring that AI adoption broadly supports equitable access and drives inclusive development.
